## Featured Reading

This episode explores one of the core invitations of the book:

**Look for lessons in everyday things.**

It begins with Homer’s memory of sitting around the family Sunday dinner table in Southeast Idaho, hearing his father encourage the family to pull wisdom from ordinary life. It continues into Charlie’s reflection on pattern recognition, metaphor, storytelling, and the responsibility to make good meaning rather than merely convenient meaning.

## Theme

Stories can save us. They can also trap us.

The difference is not whether we make meaning.

The difference is whether we make **good meaning**.
