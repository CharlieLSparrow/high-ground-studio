This episode explores legacy, family, ancestry, resilience, and the importance of understanding where you came from in order to better understand who you are and where you might be going.

It includes the story of Chub and Jack, reflections on inherited strength, and Charlie’s research-backed case for family storytelling as a source of identity, resilience, and hope.
