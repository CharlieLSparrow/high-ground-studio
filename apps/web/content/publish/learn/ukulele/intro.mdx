# Welcome to the Ukulele Hub!

If you want to learn how to play the Ukulele, you are in the right place.
The Ukulele is an incredible instrument because it is highly portable, affordable, and easy to learn.

## Your First Chords
- **C Major**: The easiest chord. Just place your ring finger on the 3rd fret of the bottom string!
- **F Major**: A step up, requiring two fingers.
