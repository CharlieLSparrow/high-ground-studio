We conducted 48 missions over the next 45 days. Days started to blend together. We would go out when it was dark then come back when it was dark. I would forget if there was a period of light in-between. Then we would sleep for a few hours and wake up and it would still be dark. Then the sun would come up and we would go out in the day and come back in the day sometimes there was a night in-between, sometimes not. I didn’t know how many days had gone by or how many missions we had done. Four hours of sleep, twelve hour mission, six hours of sleep, 16 hour mission.
