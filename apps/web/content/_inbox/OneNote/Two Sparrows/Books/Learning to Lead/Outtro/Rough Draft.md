So what’s next?  
This is the first of four books that have been rattling around in my head for nearly 20 years. I hope that it particularly reaches those of you who are discovering that leadership, like other skills, can be learned, and that whether leading yourself through a normal day or a Battalion in combat, these learned principles can make your life and the lives of others better.
 
While I am relieved to finally put thoughts to text and tell my story. I am more excited to be done so that I can work on the project I really want to get to, which is telling the story of others, from their perspective, starting with my platoon from Iraq.  
Look for “Worth The Risk” to come out next year. (2027)
 
The last book in the series will focus on my leadership experience beyond being a junior leader. To Afghanistan, which was rough for me, which is why I had to wait until now to write about it.
