# Book-Wide Story Candidate Map

## Purpose

This is a first-pass planning map for turning the Chapter Three through Ending Homer blocks into Story Candidates. It is not a final story ontology and does not edit the living manuscript.

The goal is to help Episode Playground work from the book first: Homer manuscript block -> Story Candidate -> episode sequence.

## Sizing Guide

- Under 700 words: likely one Story Candidate.
- 700 to 1,800 words: maybe 2-3 Story Candidates.
- Over 1,800 words: likely 3+ Story Candidates or multiple episodes.
