# Welcome To High Ground Odyssey

Draft status: public-facing copy draft, not wired into the website.

High Ground Odyssey is a place for learning how to stand a little steadier.

Some days that means a story. Some days it means a question. Some days it means a quiet coaching conversation with Homer when the next step has gone fuzzy around the edges.

The work is part field guide, part workshop, part campfire. We are building a system that helps people slow down, notice what matters, and choose the next honest move.

## What High Ground Odyssey Is

High Ground Odyssey is the learning side of High Ground Studio.

It is a home for:
- practical leadership lessons
- story-shaped learning
- coaching conversations
- tools for reflection
- public essays and field-guide material as the work grows

The goal is not to make life look tidy. The goal is to help good people become a little more grounded when life is doing what life does.

## What Coaching Is For

Coaching is for the moment when you do not need a speech. You need a thinking partner.

You might bring:
- a decision that keeps circling back
- a transition you are trying to walk through with some grace
- a leadership question
- a hard conversation
- a sense that something important needs more light

Coaching with Homer is meant to be simple, human, and useful. You bring the real thing. Homer helps you slow it down, sort it out, and find a next step you can stand on.

## Why The System Is Simple Right Now

The current system is intentionally simple because trust comes before machinery.

Right now, the app does the important first things:
- helps you request coaching
- gives the team a queue for follow-up
- lets the team convert a request into an appointment
- gives you simple links for calendar and contribution steps when they apply

It does not pretend to be more automated than it is. That is a feature, not a flaw. A small bridge that holds is better than a glittering bridge that drops you in the river.

## How To Request Coaching

Start at `/coaching`.

If you are not signed in, the site will ask you to sign in first. After that, you can request coaching from your dashboard.

The request form asks for:
- your preferred contact method
- an optional phone number
- an optional note

That is enough to begin.

## What Happens After You Request Coaching

After you send a request:
- the request is saved
- the internal team sees it in the coaching request queue
- Homer or the team follows up about fit, timing, and the best next step

If the team schedules a session, the appointment can appear in your dashboard with a summary and a Google Calendar add link.

What this means for you:
- you do not need to figure out the whole system
- you just need to raise your hand
- the follow-up stays human

## Donation-Supported Coaching

During this credentialing season, coaching is donation-supported.

That means there is no fixed fee up front. If the session is helpful, you can make a pay-what-you-can contribution through the external contribution link when it is available.

We do not make tax claims here. We do not treat the contribution link as a full checkout system inside the app. It is a simple external path for now.

What this means for you:
- request the conversation first
- contribute afterward if it fits your situation
- keep the amount humane and sustainable

## What Is Not Automated Yet

Some things are still intentionally manual.

Current boundaries:
- SMS is not automated
- Google Calendar is not synced through an API
- payment is not handled through full in-app Stripe Checkout
- appointment changes do not automatically update an external calendar
- a real person is still part of the follow-up

That last one is not a bug.

## How The Work Is Evolving

High Ground Odyssey is growing in layers.

First, the path needs to be clear. Then it needs to be reliable. Then it can become more graceful.

The direction is:
- clearer public field guides
- better internal team follow-up tools
- safer documentation and handoff notes
- more visible progress tracking
- careful automation only where it makes the human work better

What this means for you:
- the system will keep getting smoother
- the current version is honest about what it does
- the center of the work remains the same: learn, reflect, and take the next grounded step
